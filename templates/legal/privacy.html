Privacy Policy

At OGSP, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: name, email, profile data.
  • Account activity: courses accessed, groups joined, messages sent.
  • Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system.
  • Cookies and tracking data for analytics and site improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Secure your account and detect suspicious activity.
  • Communicate important updates, notifications, or changes.
  • Analyze trends and improve the platform user experience.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following cases:

  • With third-party service providers to help operate OGSP.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our policies.
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger or acquisition).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access control measures. However, no system is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account information.
  • Opt-out of promotional emails.
  • Request copies of your personal data.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, remember preferences, and track analytics.

7. Changes to This Policy

OGSP may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically for any changes. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Contact Us.
